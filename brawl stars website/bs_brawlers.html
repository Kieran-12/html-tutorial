Sorting

You are able to sort your brawlers by, in order of the dropdown menu:

  • Rarity
  • Rarity Descending
  • Most Trophies
  • Least Trophies
  • Closest to Next Tier
  • Power Level
  • Mastery
  • Closest to Next Mastery
  • Name
  • New
  • Class

There is also an option to sort brawlers based on if you have quests that require you to use them. When using the quest sorting, it will put the brawler with the most number of quests at the top. This is quite useful to see which brawlers you can play to finish multiple brawler quests at once.

Class

In Brawl Stars, there are classes which are used to categorise brawlers by their functions. Sometimes, these categorisations aren't the most accurate, but they provide a good explanation to new players about how the brawler should be played. There are 7 classes, which are:

  • Tanks
  • Assassins
  • Support
  • Controller
  • Damage Dealer
  • Marksmen
  • Artillery

Mastery

In Brawl Stars, Mastery, is a way of tracking the amount you have played a certian brawler. You get mastery from winning games. In 3vs3 gamemodes like brawl ball or knockout, you must win the game to get mastery points. In solo showdown, you need top 4 to get mastery points. In duo showdown, you need top 2 to get mastery points. The amount of mastery you gain is based off of the amount of trophies you had when winning. The higher the trophies, the more mastery points you will gain every win. There are way to get more mastery points than expected. The 2 methods are to:

  • Get Star Player
  • Win During Mastery Madness Event

Getting star player will give 30% more mastery than normal. Mastery Madness events increase mastery points gained by 50%, 100%, 150% or 200%.

Trophies

Trophies are a way of measuring Player's or Brawler's progress. They are awarded or lost depending on the outcome of each match. The trophies on all the brawlers combined yields the Player's total trophy count. The higher the trophies on a certain brawler, less trophies will be awarded for each win and more trophies will be lost for each loss.


The number of trophies gained and lost in each gamemode are listed below:

  • 3 vs 3
  • Solo Showdown
  • Duo Showdown

In brawl stars, the number of trophies a brawler has determines its Rank/Tier. Each, tier you reach, you are awarded 10XP. Once you reach a tier on a brawler, the number on your tier will never change, even if you lose all the trophies on the brawler. The highest tier is 35. No matter how many more trophies are earned, the tier number will never go past tier 35. Every time a brawler goes up 5 tiers, the colour of the tier symbol will change. Every 5 tiers from tier 10 onwards, bling will be awarded to the player.

Brawler Upgrades

To upgrade brawlers, you need to use coins and power points ; after upgrading brawlers to power level 7, you unlock gadgets, at power level 8, you unlock 1 gear, at power level 9, you unlock star powers, at power level 10, you unlock a 2nd gear and at power level 11, the max, you unlock hypercharges. Upgrading brawlers will also increase their stats.
